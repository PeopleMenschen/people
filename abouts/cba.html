    what do we want?

    Our desire is this and very little:

    Restitution of Damages and Stolen Rights We demand the return of all damages inflicted upon us and the restoration of the rights that have been unjustly taken away. It is imperative that justice is served, and reparations are made for the injustices endured.

    Promotion of Friendship and Unity let us foster a culture of friendship and intimacy within our community and beyond. Through mutual respect and understanding, we can build bridges that transcend divisions and promote unity among all members of society.

    Declaration of Neutrality We call upon all countries and parties involved in the misfortunes of our nation to officially and sincerely declare their neutrality. By doing so, we can reclaim our sovereignty and chart our own course, free from external interference or influence.

    Empowerment in National Service grant us the opportunity to serve our country and society through meaningful participation in its governance and general service period. By entrusting us with responsibilities, we can demonstrate to the world, and to our own nation, that the spirit of humanity and freedom still thrives within us.

    In our call for the declaration of neutrality, we seek not to sever ties, but to foster a deeper understanding of humanity's interdependence. We acknowledge the importance of maintaining economic diplomacy and friendships with all nations, yet we emphasize the paramount need for our country, nation, and society to determine our own destiny. Neutrality signifies respect for our sovereignty and the right to self-determination, enabling us to navigate our path free from external pressures and interventions. It is a declaration of our commitment to charting a course that aligns with our values and aspirations, while maintaining mutual respect and cooperation on the global stage.

    These demands encapsulate your vision for a future marked by justice, unity, and self-determination. It is through the collective pursuit of these ideals that we can strive towards a society where every individual is valued, empowered, and free to shape their own destiny.

    We must never forget and always remember fairness and equality.

    This is the only way that each person becomes the owner of her own destiny:

    In our pursuit of social justice and equality, the empowerment of disadvantaged communities, particularly women, stands as the cornerstone of our mission. Recognizing their invaluable perspectives and experiences, we prioritize their involvement in decision-making processes across all sectors. From education to infrastructure, healthcare to economic policies, the voices of the deprived society, especially women, are integral in shaping policies, programs, and initiatives that foster inclusivity and opportunity for all.

    Within the Education Department, the active participation of marginalized communities, notably women, is pivotal in crafting policies and initiatives that promote equitable access to education. By amplifying their voices, we ensure that educational systems are responsive to the diverse needs and aspirations of all learners. This inclusive approach lays the foundation for a society where every individual, regardless of background, has the opportunity to pursue their full potential and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

    Similarly, in the realms of healthcare, foreign affairs, economic development, and beyond, the engagement of disadvantaged communities, with a particular emphasis on women, is paramount. Their insights inform strategies to address systemic inequalities, foster economic empowerment, and promote sustainable development. By centering their perspectives in decision-making processes, we strive towards a future where social justice is not just an aspiration but a lived reality for all members of society.

    Talking isn't enough to solve problems. We need to put in the hard work. Words alone can't heal the pain of injustice; action is what makes a difference. So, let's focus on doing rather than just saying.


    By prioritizing the participation of disadvantaged communities, especially women, in decision-making across sectors, we reaffirm our commitment to building a more inclusive, equitable, and just society. Their voices serve as catalysts for positive change, driving policies and initiatives that uplift the marginalized and create pathways for collective prosperity. As we navigate the complexities of our shared journey towards social justice, we stand firm in our belief that true progress can only be achieved when every individual is empowered to shape their own destiny and contribute to the common good.
